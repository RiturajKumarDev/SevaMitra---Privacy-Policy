Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Welcome to SevaMitra. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when using our application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Profile Photo
  • Address, City, State, District, Division
  • User Type (Founder, Worker, Officer, SevaMitra Staff)
  • Company Name and GST Number (if applicable)
  • Department and Designation Details
  • Issue Reports and Resolution Information
  • Location related to reported issues

2. Issue Management Data

When an issue is created, assigned, resolved, approved, or rejected, the system stores information such as:

  • Issue Title
  • Issue Description
  • Issue Type
  • Priority Level
  • Issue Location
  • Assigned Worker Information
  • Resolution Notes
  • Status History
  • Timestamps related to issue workflow

3. How We Use Your Information

Collected information is used to:

  • Create and manage user accounts.
  • Process and track reported issues.
  • Assign tasks to workers.
  • Review and approve completed work.
  • Provide communication and notifications.
  • Improve application performance and services.
  • Prevent misuse, fraud, and unauthorized access.

4. Data Storage and Security

We use secure technologies and industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent users' personal information. Information may only be shared:

  • With authorized officers and administrators.
  • With assigned workers for issue resolution purposes.
  • When required by law or government authority.
  • To protect legal rights and system security.

6. User Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate information.
  • Maintain account security.
  • Do not share login credentials with others.
  • Use the platform lawfully and responsibly.

7. Data Retention

User profiles, issue records, approvals, and resolution history may be retained for operational, legal, administrative, and audit purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will become effective immediately after publication within the application.
